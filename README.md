From predictive modelling to full-scale digital transformation, we partner with businesses and institutions at every stage of their data journey.

## 🌍 About Us

Solelytics Consultancy was founded on a simple but powerful belief: that African organisations deserve world-class data and technology solutions built for their context. Headquartered in **Lagos, Nigeria**, we work with private enterprises, public institutions, and international clients who want a trusted African partner for their digital transformation initiatives.

We are not just a consultancy — we are builders. Our work spans strategy, implementation, and community impact, including a founding commitment to establish **STEM schools** that equip young Nigerians with the technical skills the continent's digital economy demands.
